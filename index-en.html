Welcome to
Lina och Alfred's
Wedding at Örtofta castle
 9th of may 2026 

We met in the initial year of our university studies and instantly bonded over a joint interest in books, board games and a variety of nerdy things. During Valborg 2019 we became an official couple and now approximately 7 years later we invite you to the next step in our big journey!

The Weddingday will take place at Örtofta Slott. The day will begin with the wedding ceremony at Örtofta church. After that we will move to Örtofta Slott for the wedding reception. The reception will start with some mingle, and a toast for the bride and groom, and pastries will be served. Later in the day we will move to the dining area for a three-course meal. During the dinner we encourage speeches or other performances from you all. If this is something you wish to do there are more information on how to contact the toastmasters further down. After the dinner the dance floor and the bar will be opened. We hope that you can all come and celebrate our big day with us.

We ask you to please RSVP no later than the last January using the link below. If you already know if you can come or not we would appreciate if you RSVPd earlier.

The Wedding day

The full Wedding Day will take place at Örfota slott and Örtofta church, with a few hundred meters between them.
The Wedding Ceremony will start at 14:00 at Örtofta church. Please arrive before that so you have time to enter and take a seat before the start of the Ceremony.
At around 15:30 the toast to the bride and groom will be held, and after that all activities will be held at Örtofta Slott.
If you want to give a speech or have any other type of performance during the dinner please contact our toastmasters. There contact details are published furthest down on the website.
If you can only attend parts of the day please contact us to receive a more detailed schedule.

Dress code

The dress code for the wedding is "Formal"
A good definition of Formal can be found here. As this is a spring wedding we encourage everyone to wear bright colors, even the men, regardless of the fact that that is generally not allowed within the dress code.

Travel Information

It is easiest to travel to Örtofta Slott using either a car or the train.
For the people traveling by car there are parking areas both at the venue and at the church.
For the people traveling by train exit the train at Örtofta station and then you can walk a short distance to the venue through “Lyktornas Park”
For the people who are traveling from afar there are a couple of rooms at the venue available. They cost 1200(single room)-1450(double room) per night. They are available for the following nights: Friday-Saturday, Saturday-Sunday or both nights. Check in time on Saturday is at 13:00, and if you desire an early Check in that costs 300 and then you can Check in at 9:00. If you arrive on Friday the check in time is at 15:00. Breakfast is also included. If you wish to reserve a room please contact us directly.
